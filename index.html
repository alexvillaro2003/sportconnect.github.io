Find Your Team. Play More.

SportConnect is a social web platform for university students with a SportCard. Create activities, join games, chat with teammates, and build a sports community on campus.

The Problem
No one to play with

University students have access to free sports facilities through their SportCard — but finding people to play with is frustratingly hard. There's no central tool, no community, just missed opportunities.

🔥
The Solution
One platform, all sports

SportConnect brings students together. Create or join sports activities, chat with your group, track your stats, unlock achievements, and discover people who share your passion for sport on campus.

What we built

Every Feature You Need

A focused set of tools designed to make university sports social, organized and fun.

🔐
SportCard Login

Registration requires a valid university SportCard code. Only real students can join — keeping the community trusted and campus-focused.

📋
Activity Board

Browse all open sports activities, filter by sport or date, and join with a single click. Open games or request-based access — your call as organizer.

💬
Group Chat

Every activity gets its own real-time group chat via SignalR, stored in MongoDB and automatically deleted 24 hours after the activity ends.

📊
Personal Stats

Track your most played sport, total activities joined, different sports practiced, and how many different players you've teamed up with.

🏅
Achievements

Unlock badges as you play more and engage with the community. Visual, motivating and automatically evaluated every time you join or create an activity.

🔍
Find Players

Search for other students by name, see their public profile with stats, achievements, languages and a short bio. Build your sports network.

Under the Hood

Tech Stack

Two databases, one real-time layer — each chosen for the right reasons.

SQL — The Source of Truth

Users & SportCards
Auth, profiles, nationality, languages, roles
Activities
Sport, date, capacity, mode (public / request)
Participations
Who joined what — confirmed, pending or rejected
Achievements
Logros table + UsuarioLogros with unlock date

MongoDB — The Chat Engine

Messages Collection
activityId, userId, username, text, sentAt
TTL Index
Auto-deletes messages 24h after activity ends — no manual cleanup needed
Compound Index
activityId + sentAt for instant message history retrieval
SignalR Hub
Real-time delivery to all connected participants
ASP.NET Core C# SQL Server MongoDB SignalR JWT Auth ASP.NET Identity Razor Pages
